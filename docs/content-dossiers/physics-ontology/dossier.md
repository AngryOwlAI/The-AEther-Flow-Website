## Claim boundaries and forbidden implications

### Claim boundaries

- Ontology vocabulary can orient readers.
- Mathematical recovery and prediction remain downstream.
- Registered TeX and claim registries remain authoritative.
- PDFs are generated human-readable derivatives.
- Explanatory Markdown and generated explainers remain downstream from TeX
  authority.

### Forbidden implications

- Do not claim ontology alone forces GR.
- Do not promote ontology from generated pages.
- Do not describe AEther-flow as an ordinary fluid moving through observed
  space.
- Do not treat exact-GR benchmark compatibility as a completed
  first-principles derivation.
- Do not treat PDFs, validators, public pages, or registry metadata as
  scientific proof.
