   derivations.
2. Route sequence: selector, construction, audit, Refuter stress, frozen
   negative status, source-extension continuation.
3. Mechanism: explicit toy tags made a partial response relation possible;
   tag-removal collapsed the relation to `X_empty`.

   impossibility.

## Claim boundaries and forbidden implications

### Claim boundaries

- The explicit-tag-only finite toy route is frozen locally.
- The freeze records a scoped obstruction, not a general verdict against the
  project.
- The construction and audit remain `draft/control` evidence.
- Later source-extension work remains separate from the frozen route.

### Forbidden implications

- A finite toy model derived a physical metric.
- Toy tags became canonical ontology.
- The route adopted `Resp_lc`, `M_src`, `MetricData(E)`, `g_eff`, matter
  coupling, Einstein equations, benchmark promotion, or completed derivation.
- The result proves future source-extension impossibility.
- The result is a global theory rejection.
