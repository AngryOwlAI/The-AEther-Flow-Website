## Claim boundaries and forbidden implications

### Claim boundaries

- The hub can recommend route order.
- The hub can state audience-specific caution.
- The hub can point reviewers to prerequisites before PG-026.
- The hub can link source/provenance surfaces as audit evidence.

### Forbidden implications

- The hub creates new scientific, mathematical, AI, operational, or reviewer
  claims.
- The hub updates current source state.
- A guided reading path proves `MetricData(E)`, `g_eff`, matter coupling,
  Einstein equations, benchmark promotion, or completed derivation.
- A reviewer prerequisite section is the PG-026 reviewer packet.
