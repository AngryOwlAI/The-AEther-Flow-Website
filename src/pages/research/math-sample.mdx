This page is a rendering sample for the website scaffold. The equations below
are standard notation examples, not new AEther Flow research claims.

Inline math renders through KaTeX, for example $E = mc^2$ and
$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} = \rho / \varepsilon_0$.

Display math should remain readable and horizontally scroll when needed:

$$
G_{\mu\nu} + \Lambda g_{\mu\nu}
= \frac{8\pi G}{c^4} T_{\mu\nu}
$$

The Phase 1 acceptance target is narrow:

- MDX pages build.
- Inline and display equations render.
- The page keeps its claim status explicit.
- The website remains a presentation layer.
